Pizza Recipe

This is a Magharita Pizza!

It is made with cheese and tomato sauce. As you take a bit the cheese strecthes and gives you a perfectly balanced taste!

  • Cheese
  • Tomatos
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Water

Steps

  1. Pour water into the flour and knead it
  2. Let the dough rest
  3. Then roll out the dough into a large circle
  4. Make a tomato sauce
  5. Spread the tomato sauce on the rolled dough
  6. Put slices of cheese everywhere on the dough
  7. Put this into an oven for 25 minutes
